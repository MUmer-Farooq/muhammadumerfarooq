E-Commerce Sales Analysis

Overview: This project delivers an end-to-end analysis of an e-commerce store with over 2 million transactions. The Power BI dashboard provides real-time insights into sales performance, customer behavior, top-selling products, and operational inefficiencies. It enables business teams to monitor trends, discover growth opportunities, and make data-driven decisions that resulted in a 23% increase in revenue within the analyzed period.

  • Sales Performance Analysis: Tracked monthly and daily sales trends, revenue contribution, average order value (AOV), highest-selling categories, and seasonal buying patterns. Identified ₹21M total sales and calculated customer lifetime value (CLV).
  • Category Performance Breakdown: Compared category-wise performance across Sets, Kurtas, Western Dress, Saree, Ethnic Wear, and Accessories. Identified top revenue-driving categories and underperformers.
  • Sales Channel Analysis: Evaluated performance across Amazon, Flipkart, Meesho, Myntra, and website orders. Determined channels with the highest conversion and return rates.
  • Customer Demographics: Segmented customers into Adult, Teen, and Senior Citizen groups. Analyzed age-wise buying behavior, preferred categories, and repeat purchase rate.
  • Geographical Insights: Identified top-performing Indian states such as Uttar Pradesh, Telangana, Tamil Nadu, West Bengal, and Maharashtra. Highlighted high-conversion and low-performing cities for targeted marketing.
  • Order Status & Operations Tracking: Monitored delivered, returned, cancelled, and refunded orders. Diagnosed causes of high return rates and identified logistics bottlenecks.
  • Size & Inventory Trends: Analyzed demand for clothing sizes (XS to XXL) to improve inventory planning. Helped reduce out-of-stock issues by identifying fast-moving size variants.

  • Power BI (DAX, Power Query, Modeling)
  • Business Analytics & KPI Measurement
  • ETL and Data Cleaning
  • Data Transformation using Power Query
  • Dashboard Design & User Experience (UX)
  • Sales & Marketing Analytics
  • Customer Segmentation & Cohort Analysis

  • ₹21,000,000+ Total Sales Analyzed
  • 23% Revenue Growth after insights implementation
  • 31,000+ Unique Customers
  • 691 Average Order Value (AOV)
  • 2,000,000+ Transactions processed
  • Top 5 States contributed 42% of total sales
  • Return Rate Reduction: 14% → 9% after operational insights

Covid-19 Dashboard

Overview: This project focuses on analyzing the global impact of the COVID-19 pandemic using an interactive Power BI dashboard. The goal was to track confirmed cases, recoveries, and deaths across different countries and WHO regions, while identifying monthly patterns and trends. The dashboard provides real-time insights to help policymakers, healthcare professionals, and the public understand the progression and impact of COVID-19 globally.

  • Global Insights: Analyzed 829M+ confirmed cases, 388M recoveries, and 43M deaths worldwide. Identified major hotspots and trends by region.
  • Comparative Country Analysis: Visualized country-wise case distribution for US, Brazil, India, and other countries using bar charts for confirmed, recovered, and death cases.
  • Tracking Trends: Line charts were used to monitor daily new cases, recoveries, and deaths over time to identify waves and peaks.
  • Interactive Filters & Drilldowns: Enabled filtering by country, WHO region, and time period to allow detailed exploration of the data.
  • Visual Storytelling: Used pie charts and comparative visuals to clearly present recovery vs. active vs. death cases for better comprehension.
  • Monthly & Regional Patterns: Highlighted monthly case trends and regional differences to guide decision-making and resource allocation.
